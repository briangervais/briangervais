About Brian

Brian has 10+ years of experience working on Drupal, Wordpress, and Shopify sites. He has a Bachelor's degree in Psychology from the University of Nevada and a certificate in Web Development from Portland Community College.

He is proud to have worked with Aten Design Group, Spire Digital, and Oxbow Labs. If you are looking to work with an agency, these each come highly recommended.

If you're looking for a freelancer, Brian is your man.

Brian is an avid proponent of education. He teaches classes for the Manitou Springs School District in his home state of Colorado. He will be teaching a Website Graphic Design course to high school students in 2019.
